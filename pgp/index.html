What not to send

No channel listed on this page is an authorized system for classified national security information, controlled unclassified information subject to a contractual handling requirement, or export-controlled technical data. Encryption is not authorization.

If an engagement requires handling of controlled material, the handling method and authorized environment are established in the engagement agreement before any transfer occurs. Where counsel is involved, privileged material should move through counsel.

Do Not Transmit

  • Classified information
  • CUI under contractual handling requirements
  • ITAR / EAR-controlled technical data
  • Privileged material outside counsel
  • Personal health information
